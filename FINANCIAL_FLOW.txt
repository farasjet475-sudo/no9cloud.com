FINANCIAL FLOW SUMMARY - VERSION 2

1. RECEIPT / CASH SALE
- Save to sales + sale_items
- Deduct stock
- Record stock movement OUT
- Post finance sale entry

2. QUOTATION
- No database save
- No stock movement
- No finance journal
- PDF only

3. INVOICE / CREDIT SALE
- Save to invoices + invoice_items
- Deduct stock
- Record stock movement OUT
- Record customer balance / receivable
- Post finance receivable entry if finance helper exists

4. EXPENSE
- Save to expenses
- Post expense finance entry

5. REPORTING
- Inventory reports read products + stock_movements
- Financial statement pages read journal tables when present
- If journal tables are missing, pages fall back to sales / invoices / expenses / products
